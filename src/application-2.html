IAPSC Membership

The International Association of Professional Security Consultants (IAPSC) serves you, the security consultant, by helping you grow and successfully manage your business. As a member, you’ll receive a member directory listing, partnering opportunities, and educational services from the most respected and widely recognized consulting association in the security industry.

Before applying, be sure to review the Membership Qualifications and the Benefits of Membership.

Annual Dues:

IAPSC membership runs annually from January 1 to December 31. The annual membership dues for IAPSC members are $445 per year. If you become a member mid-year, the dues are pro-rated accordingly for your first year.

Application Fee:

The IAPSC membership application and review process is thorough and comprehensive, including a background investigation for each applicant. In order to help cover these costs, IAPSC charges a $200 membership application fee.
